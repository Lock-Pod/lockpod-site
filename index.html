Secure your bike—peace of mind included.

LockPod is committed to providing secure and sustainable micromobility storage systems where they are needed most.

Security

We understand the importance of secure bike storage. Bicycles can be a significant investment and riders deserve peace of mind when leaving their bikes as they visit your business. LockPods are designed to prevent thieves from seeing or tampering with bikes inside, providing maximum security for bike owner as they shop and work at your business.

Aesthetics

LockPod is designed to enhance not only the security options but the beauty of their surroundings, standing out while also complementing the areas in which they are installed. Micromobility options promote a greener planet, and the infrastructure to support it should promote the planet's beauty as well.

Convenience

LockPod is focused on making the locking experience as convenient as possible for riders, and this involves eliminating the need to bring a key and lock everywhere. LockPods require only a smartphone with an active internet connection to unlock, ensuring that they are ready to use for almost all riders when they are needed.
